Examples


In this Block, we ask you to make your guess about the true state in each round. There are 20 rounds in total. You will see the following decision page in a round:



Payment

Every correct guess from a group member will mean £2 is added to the payment for each group member, every incorrect guess from a group member will mean £0 is added to the payment for each group member.

Example:
The true state is RED. Two group members correctly guessed state RED, and one group member incorrectly guessed state BLUE. Consequently, your payment would be £2x2+£0x1=£4
