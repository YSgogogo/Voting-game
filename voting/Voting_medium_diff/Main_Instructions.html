Structure of the experiment

  • You will face 10 rounds in this experiment.
  • Each round is independent of other rounds.
  • Each round is equally likely to be selected for your payment.
  • You might be matched with different participants as group members in each round.
  • Thus, it is important for you to take each round seriously.

Structure of the round

Stage 1: Information

There is a state called the ''unknown state'' that is filled with multiple balls with different colors in two boxes. The state is either the ''RED'', including two boxes: Box A and Box B, or the ''BLUE'', including two boxes: Box A and Box B. As you can see in the following Figure, the ''RED'' has more red balls, and the ''BLUE'' has more blue balls in each box.




  • Every 3 participants in this lab are randomly selected into one group in the beginning of each round.
  • We will roll a die and determine the ''unknown state'' - either the ''RED'' or ''BLUE''. No one will be told which one is chosen!
  • You are asked to select a card to reveal a box from the chosen state:
  • From the chosen state and your selected box, you are asked to select a ball to reveal its color:
  • The color of the ball (not Box A or Box B!) you and your group members got will be shown to everyone in your group.
  • e.g., if you got a red ball from Box A, and your group members got a red ball and a blue ball, you will be shown the following:


Stage 2: Who you want to connect with

In this stage, you can gain more information by connecting with at most one voter in your group. How you can connect with other group members depends on your choice and other group members choices in the following task.

  • You are asked to rank whom you want to connect with from the most to the least. You will see the following interface:




  • The computer will match you with other group members based on your ranking, i.e. the computer will match you first with the group member you put in the first box, then with the one in the second box, and so on until the last box.
  • So it is IMPORTANT for you to rank exactly based on who you want to connect with from the most to the least.
  • If you are not successfully connected to any voters in your group, you just wait in the page for one minute. You will see the following screenshot:




  • If you are successfully connected to another participant, you can (I) see he/she got a ball from which box: either Box A or Box B; (II) freely to chat with him/her for 1 minute. You will see the following screenshot:




    • Stage 3: Voting

    • Now you need to vote about which state is chosen in stage 1: RED or BLUE.




    • Your payoff is determined by the number of correct votes: If there are majority (2 or 3) participants in your group vote correctly, each of you in the group will earn £15！ If there are 0 or 1 participant in your group vote correctly, each of you in the group will only earn £2!
